    E-Summit

    E-Summit is the second biggest of its kind in Eastern India after IIT Kharagpur. Boasting of a plethora of workshops, competitions, discussion panels, business pitches and guest lecture series, the summit aims to help students get exposure about entrepreneurship, encourage students to take up ventures in career and to help build an active startup ecosystem in the country. The mission of the summit is to educate and support student entrepreneurs, startups and SMEs for creating high-value jobs.

    Events

    During E-Summit, we host a series of entrepreneurial events. Workshops, competitions, discussion panels, business pitches and E-Talks are conducted. The main objective behind these events is to cultivate entrepreneurial qualities in students and motivate them to dream big.

    Boardroom

    It is a notable event where one can get the actual feel of the corporate world. The strategy, tension and the excitement will help your pseudo company to reach heights. It is a perfect platform to build a business mind, confront real world business crisis and find innovative ideas to tackle it.

    E-Talk

    Entrepreneurship-Talks are as series of interactive sessions where we invite iconic personalities to give insight into different entrepreneurial topics and their journey of becoming an entrepreneur. Ever since its debut, the E-Summit of NIT Durgapur has hosted a wide array of incredible talents.

    Pitchfest

    It is a flagship event that depicts the very essence of Entrepreneurship Development Cell. Through this event we provide a platform where one can pitch their B-Plan, which includes funding, space, community and services to create their dream company.

    YouTuber, popularly known by her YouTube channel’s name “MostlySane”

    We had received the honour of her presence during E-Summit 2019. As a result of a jam-packed arena, and an overwhelming onslaught of genuine questions, the session with Ms Koli was built up on questions from the youngsters in the crowd, which ranged from technical questions, to Ms Koli's perspective on the reason behind her fame.

    Mr. Rahul Kaushik

    Founder of "The Melting Words"

    Ms Shaan Suhas Kumar

    Represented India at the Miss Earth 2017 beauty pageant

It enlightened young minds and highly inspired them to follow their dreams.
